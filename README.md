Customer Segmentation is a data analytics project that groups customers into different segments based on their purchasing behavior, annual income, spending score, and demographic characteristics. The project helps businesses better understand their customers and develop targeted marketing strategies.

Objectives:

Identify distinct customer groups.
Analyze customer purchasing patterns.
Improve targeted marketing campaigns.
Support business decision-making through data-driven insights.

Tools & Technologies:
Power BI
Python (Optional)
Data Visualization
Clustering Techniques

Dataset Features:
Customer ID
Gender
Age
Annual Income

Key Features
Customer clustering and segmentation
Interactive dashboards and visualizations
Spending behavior analysis
Income-based customer grouping
Business insights and recommendations

Expected Outcomes
Identification of high-value customers
Better understanding of customer behavior
Improved marketing efficiency
Data-driven customer targeting
